About Me

My name is Emily Rodriguez and I am a junior Web Developer and former journalist. I decided to make the change from journalism to programming thanks to the evolving technical aspects of the communications field. I began coding to pick up a few skills to give me a competitive edge but ended up falling in love with it. I enrolled in Codeup, a 16-week developer bootcamp to enhance my skills and change my career path. The only thing better then seeing your name in print after completing a hard-earned story is seeing my code come to life. I have a passion for creating positive user experiences and make it a point to ensure ease of use and understanding. My understanding of both front end and back end development guarantees technical feasibility of UI/UX designs and that my code is and optimized for speed and scalability.
